# Privacy Policy for yaabsa

**Effective Date:** May 15, 2026

Welcome to **yaabsa**. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information and data are handled when you use the yaabsa application across all supported platforms (Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux).

Because yaabsa is designed as an unofficial client to connect to your own self-hosted Audiobookshelf server, our approach to privacy is simple: **your data stays entirely between your device and your personal server.** We do not collect, monetize, or transmit your personal data to our own servers.

---

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect

yaabsa is built to respect your privacy. As the developer of yaabsa, I do not have access to any of the data you use within the app. Specifically:

* **No Centralized Telemetry:** We do not track your app usage, crash logs, or navigation habits centrally.
* **No Account Data:** We do not collect or have access to your username, password, or the URL of your Audiobookshelf server.
* **No Media Tracking:** We do not track what audiobooks, podcasts, or eBooks you are reading, listening to, or searching for.

## 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide a seamless experience, yaabsa stores certain necessary information directly on the device you are using:

* **Encrypted Credentials:** The authentication token required to connect to your Audiobookshelf server is saved locally and securely encrypted on your device.
* **App Settings:** User preferences, UI customizations, and player configurations are stored locally.
* **Downloaded Media & Cache:** Any media you choose to download for offline access (such as audio files, ePUBs, PDFs, and annotations) or cache for smoother playback is stored locally on your device's storage.

## 3. Communication with Your Server

yaabsa acts as a bridge between your device and the Audiobookshelf server you specify.

* All synchronization (listening progress, play history, downloaded files, and server management tasks) occurs directly between your app and your server.
* The security of this connection (e.g., HTTPS/SSL) and the privacy of the server itself depend on how you have configured your personal Audiobookshelf instance.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Image Fetching

While yaabsa connects directly to your server, there are specific server management features that may interact with third-party networks:

* **Metadata and Image Matching:** If you use the "Match items" feature to pull metadata or cover art, the app or your connected Audiobookshelf server may fetch images and data from external providers (such as Google Books, the Amazon CDN, or other configurable metadata providers). These are configurable by the Administrator of your Audiobookshelf server, and you can choose yourself which providers to use or not use.
* **IP Address Exposure:** When your device directly loads these images or fetches this data from third-party networks, those third-party services may log your IP address and process the request according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Since yaabsa is an actively maintained personal project, this Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect new features or changes in how the app handles local data. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this document. It's your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how your data is handled. Any significant changes will be highlighted in the release notes of the app updates.
